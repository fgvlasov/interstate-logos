Business Logo Fabrication

One Stop Shop

The business logo fabrication division of Interstate Logos, Interstate Graphics, was established in 1991 and has grown to be the largest fabricator of business logos in the country. Interstate Graphics provides continuity for the quality and content of business logos and allows greater control over the time required for design, fabrication, and delivery to ultimately expedite installation along the roadway.

Free Graphic Design

Our certified graphic design team is ready to assist you in developing a design for your business logos that is legible and utilizes colors that have proven the most effective for visibility. We currently have access to more than 40,000 business logo art files and 1,200 unique pieces of corporate approved business logo artwork.

Quality & Service

All business logos are guaranteed to meet the associated State standards and specifications. Our fabrication process follows to-the-letter the recommended procedures for the application and treatment of the retroreflective sheeting. Each business logo is screen printed by hand to ensure the accuracy and vibrancy of the colors, as well as the durability of the product.
